About

"A Life Of Crime Film Festival" is focused on four different crime film genres: Mafiosos, Psychos, Outlaws, and Thieves.

The audience will experience american crime classics at their finest. The featured films have shaped the history of american crime film-making and still continue to influence film-making today.


Director Highlights


"A Life Of Crime Film Festival" is showing films directed by Francis Ford Coppola and Steven Soderbergh. Francis Ford Coppola rose to prominence with the release in 1972 of "The Godfather" and Steven Soderbergh received international recognition through the films "Erin Brochovic" and the remake of "Ocean's Eleven".
